Welcome to the Faculty of Engineering at Aliko Dangote University of Science & Technology (ADUSTEC), Wudil. Our faculty is committed to excellence in engineering education, research, and community service.

Established with a vision to produce world-class engineers, our faculty offers comprehensive programs across various engineering disciplines. We combine theoretical knowledge with practical skills to prepare our students for the challenges of the modern engineering landscape.

Our state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty members, and industry partnerships provide students with a conducive learning environment. We emphasize innovation, critical thinking, and ethical practices in all our engineering programs.

At ADUSTEC Faculty of Engineering, we are dedicated to producing graduates who will contribute significantly to technological advancement and industrial development in Nigeria and beyond.

ENGINEERING DEPARTMENTS

Agricultural Engineering

Our Agricultural Engineering program focuses on applying engineering principles to agricultural production and processing. Students learn about farm machinery, irrigation systems, food processing, and sustainable agricultural practices.

Automotive Engineering

The Automotive Engineering program covers the design, development, and manufacturing of vehicles. Students gain expertise in vehicle dynamics, powertrain systems, automotive electronics, and modern vehicle technologies.

Civil Engineering

Our Civil Engineering program prepares students to design, construct, and maintain infrastructure projects. The curriculum includes structural engineering, transportation systems, water resources, and environmental engineering.

Electrical Engineering

The Electrical Engineering program covers the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. Students learn about power systems, control systems, telecommunications, and electronic circuit design.

Mechanical Engineering

Our Mechanical Engineering program focuses on the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems. Students study thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, materials science, and mechanical design.

Mechatronics Engineering

The Mechatronics Engineering program combines mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering. Students learn about robotics, automation, control systems, and intelligent systems design.

Water Resources Engineering

Our Water Resources Engineering program focuses on the management and development of water resources. Students learn about hydrology, water treatment, irrigation systems, and water conservation techniques.
